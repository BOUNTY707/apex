TO PARTICIPANTS

Students of the elementary (3-4 grades), middle and senior (5 – 11 classes) of general education schools, students of secondary special educational institutions from the Republic of Turkey, Russia and other CIS countries take part in the Olympiad. The age of participants in the Olympics is from 9 to 19 years.

GROUP LEADER 10 children (and more) – FREE accommodation and meals in a 5 * hotel.

ORGANIZERS

The International Olympiad for schoolchildren and students in Antalya is an annual open cognitive event organized by the Academy of Tourism in Antalya, the International Russian School EDUANT, with the support of the Consulate General of the Russian Federation in Antalya, the City Hall of Antalya, as well as leading media in Turkey, Russia, Kazakhstan .

The International Academy of Tourism in Antalya and the International Russian School in Antalya are part of the International Educational Corporation EDUANT (Academy of Tourism in Antalya, International Russian School in Antalya, kindergarten “Limpopo”, Eduant Video production studio).

The Corporation is the organizer of large-scale international cultural and educational projects, including the International Festival of Tourism, Culture and Sport, the Total Dictation, the TOEFL IBT International Test, etc.

TO LEARN MORE HIDE

The International Academy of Tourism in Antalya is a recognized leader in education in the field of tourism and hospitality. Training is conducted in English and Russian languages ​​according to international programs from the Educational Institute of the US Hotel Association (EI-AH & LA). Graduates of the Academy receive three international diplomas – American, European and Turkish. The institution has international accreditation from the Institute of Hospitality (UK), and is included in the list of 100 best hospitality schools in Europe by the version of Eurhodip. International Academy of Tourism Has a membership in the World Tourist Organization WTO at the United Nations.

The International Russian School EDUANT in Antalya is the first educational institution in Turkey and one of the first in the world to teach in Russian. The school is licensed by the Ministry of Education of the Republic of Turkey and works under the program “School of Russia”, approved as an educational standard by the Ministry of Science and Education of the Russian Federation. Graduates receive Turkish diplomas and Russian secondary school diplomas. The International Russian School is rightfully proud of the Order of Merit for Society of the 1st degree, the Diploma for raising the prestige of the Russian Federation abroad and contributing to the development and promotion of Russian culture and education outside the Russian Federation, the high results of the expert assessment of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation and the Center for Independent Assessment of the quality of education and educational audit of the Russian Federation.
